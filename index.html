Overview

This interactive tool lets you visualize the FCFS, SCAN, and CSCAN disk scheduling algorithms in action. Adjust parameters, view real-time metrics, and see how scheduling decisions impact performance.

First-Come-First-Serve (FCFS)

Processes requests as they arrive. Ideal for basic scenarios but may result in longer seek times when requests are scattered.

SCAN (Elevator Algorithm)

Moves the disk head back and forth, fulfilling requests in both directions. Balances efficiency and fairness.

Circular SCAN (CSCAN)

Processes requests in one direction, resetting to the start. Ensures predictable processing times but may involve more head movement.
